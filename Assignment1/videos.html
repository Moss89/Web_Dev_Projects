Videos.

Dancing through Japan!

Dance Dance Revolution (DDR) is an extremely popular arcade game in Japan, with many people trying to be both the fastest and the most stylish dancer on the floor. This particular arcade was in Tokyo, where a particularly "fluid" gentleman showed off his skills.

River adventure in Yunnan, China

Yunnan, in South West China, is known to have Spring weather all year round. It is also very close to Vietnam, sharing many its the physical characteristics. This particular trip was along the Lacang River.

Chinese Opera singing

Chinese Opera, or Peking Opera, first emerged in the 18th century. The singers hit VERY high notes, and its definitely an acquired taste! The vast majority of performances are in theatres, but luckily I had to chance to see a street side performance!

A little bit of Ireland in Shanghai, China

The Irish embassy regularly holds events to promote Ireland, Irish tourism, and Irish products. This particular event had Irish music and dancing. Companies such as Kerry Group were also in attendance, giving samples of Irish dairy products.

Peace and quiet in a Kyoto Zen Garden, Japan

Zen gardens are beautifully and thoughtfully arranged gardens consisting mainly of raked sand and rocks. Some plants are often added to. The gardens are meant to help with meditation, and this particular one was in Ryoanji Temple, Kyoto, which is the most famous zen garden in the country!
